About Me

I am a dedicated software engineer, born with a fervent passion for technology that was ignited at a very young age. Growing up in a family involved in the computer retail business, I was exposed to computers early on. My first real sense of achievement came when, as a curious and slightly rebellious elementary school student, I managed to bypass a password my mother had set to limit my gaming. That thrilling night, successfully cracking the password and playing games, sparked my initial fascination with technology and its workings.

This burgeoning interest was further fueled by my grandfather, a pivotal figure in our family business. He had developed an ERP system using C/C++, and through his programming textbooks and the software he created, I gained deep insights into the practical applications of programming. Additionally, playing chess with him taught me that all decisions involve logical reasoning, much like the logic found in programming languages.

As I grew, my passion for technology only deepened. In high school, I gravitated towards the Information Science department, where interacting with peers and professionals in the field confirmed my talent for logical reasoning. This realization not only affirmed my early experiences but also solidified my career path in software engineering.

Today, my professional journey is marked by continuous learning and innovative problem-solving. I contribute to projects that have a global impact, reducing system errors, optimizing APIs, and developing scalable architectures across various platforms, from cloud services like AWS and Azure to technologies such as Docker and Kubernetes. Despite the personal challenges that come my way, my commitment to software development remains unwavering, as I continue to inspire those around me with the solutions I craft and the unique perspective I bring to the tech community.
